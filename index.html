Forsaken Characters

Shedletsky

Shedletsky is one of the characters in Forsaken. He is a sentinel Survivor and the first free sentinel available to all players. He uses two iconic tools: his sword that stuns Killers for a few seconds, and a fried chicken leg (actually a turkey leg) that grants Regeneration.

Guest666

Guest666 is feared for his Blood Hunt ability, turning matches into genuine horror. He applies pressure with darkness, intimidation, and aggressive map control.

Noli

Noli is a chaotic anomaly known for glitch energy, reality distortion, and unpredictable attacks. He destabilizes players with sudden teleports and visual corruption.

Guest1337

Guest1337 is an elite variant of Guest abilities, using enhanced stealth, precision strikes, and high-level control. His combat pattern is faster, sharper, and harder to counter.
