Forsaken Characters

Shedletsky

Shedletsky is one of the characters in Forsaken.Shedletsky is a sentinel Survivor as well as the first free sentinel available to all players. He uses two of his iconic tools: his sword, which can stun Killers for a few seconds, and a fried chicken leg (Which, believe it or not, is actually a turkey leg), which gives him Regeneration.

Guest666

Guest666 is a killer in Forsaken.Guest 666 , otherwise better known as Sixer, appears in a Letter and a Photo in the Lobby. They are one of the six playable Killers in FORSAKEN and are the most expensive Killer to unlock, costing 1,666 Player Points . Guest 666 was released in the Chapter 2: Strangers from the Past update on Halloween night, October 31, 2025 (November 1 in some regions), alongside Veeronica. A bloodthirsty demon who thrives on attrition and precision, Guest 666 wears down Survivors over time and gains Blood with each kill. At full power, they can plunge the map into darkness while their attack damage increases significantly
